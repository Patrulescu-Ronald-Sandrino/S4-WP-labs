^About

Star Wars is a series of SF movies, along with the franchise it became, about space and space conflict which started with the 1977 film written and directed by George Lucas. Various media contributed to the dimensional growth of the franchise.

All 9 films of the Skywalker Saga received nomination in the Academy Awards. They, along with the 2 anthology films: Rouge One (2016) and Solo (2018) made the franchise the second-highest grossing film franchise of all time.
